1.读书报告: 学生听讲课录音之前，阅读相关章节内容，并如实记录读书所用的时间/时长、实际阅读范围和300字左右总结(可以包括阅读感想、读书带来的新收获、新的问题等)，写成报告，直接发布到系统里面。

2.出勤: 实际听课并做笔记，总结讲课内容。

3.讨论: 第三讲、第七讲和第十讲之后分别有一次讨论，全学期一共三次讨论。学生必须在Populi讨论室里面回答讨论问题，并积极回应其他同学的帖子。

4.两次考试: 期中考试涵盖第1-5周的阅读和讲课内容;期末考试涵盖整个10周的阅读和讲课内容。

5.小组释经报告: 正确的圣经解释能适用在更大的信仰群体中。释经时一个人的洞察应能与其他人的洞察保持一致、相互平衡、互为补充。这个项目需要以小组为单位，通过会议商讨解释圣经。学生将以3-5人为一组进行合作(鼓励同城学生最好能见面一起学习交流。若建立小组有困难，请写信给老 师商讨解决方案)，并制作一份5页(正文宋体五号单倍行距，6000字左右)的报告，总结他们对一段圣经文本的一致解释。

  1. 组建释经小组: 2019年10月26日

  3. 提交小组释经报告: 2019年12月14日(按时完成作业会得到额外分数。请大家安排好时间。)

A. VIDEO MATERIALS & WEEKLY ASSIGNMENT (35%)

All students are required to study the video material in populi system. Afterwards, students should hand in the weekly assignment by the deadline stated in the system.

Weekly assignment request student to write summary of the video content, fill in facts, and specific questions.

B. ATTENDANCE & DISCUSSION (35%)

All students are required to attend onsite discussion based on the video materials in populi system. All students are required to make their own opinions, as well as active responses to instructor and students.

For discussion session, students will be given questions and discussion topics according to the last lesson. Students should prepare before the discussion and actively participate in it with response to other students.

C. FINAL PAPER (30%) / BIBLE READING JOURNAL (30%)

Each student should prepare an 8-10 page exegesis paper. The exegesis paper should begin by discussing what the passage meant in its original setting and then describe what the passage means to a modern ministry setting. The project should reflect learning that has taken place during the quarter, including sound hermeneutical techniques. Detailed instructions will be provided for this paper. Feel free to discuss ideas for your paper with the professor at any time throughout the quarter.
